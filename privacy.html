2. Overview

Ludwigs Mine is an offline game. The app itself does not collect, store or transmit any personal data to us. Save games are stored locally on your device only. There are no user accounts, no tracking by us, and no servers receiving your data.

To fund the game, the app displays advertising via Google AdMob. The AdMob SDK may process data (see section 4).

3. Local storage

Your progress, discoveries and settings are stored locally in the app's storage on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. It is deleted when you uninstall the app or start a new game.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Provider: Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland.

To serve ads, Google may process:

  • Your device's advertising ID (Android Advertising ID / iOS IDFA)
  • IP address (truncated, for coarse location)
  • Device and diagnostic information (device type, OS version, crash and performance data)
  • Ad interactions (impressions, clicks)

Purpose: serving ads, fraud prevention, reach measurement and — only with your consent — personalised advertising.

Legal basis: personalised ads rely on your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), obtained through Google's consent dialog (UMP) on first launch. Non-personalised ads rely on our legitimate interest in funding the app (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

Withdrawing consent: in-game via Menu → "Privacy settings". If you decline, the game remains fully playable; only non-personalised ads or no ads will be served.

More information: policies.google.com/privacy · AdMob privacy

5. In-app purchase "Remove ads"

The transaction is handled by the respective store (Google Play / Apple App Store). We receive no payment data, only an anonymous purchase confirmation. After purchase, the ad SDK is no longer initialised.

6. Recipients and third countries

When serving ads, Google may process data in third countries (including the USA), relying on appropriate safeguards (EU Standard Contractual Clauses / EU-US Data Privacy Framework).

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access (Art. 15), rectification (Art. 16), erasure (Art. 17), restriction (Art. 18), data portability (Art. 20) and objection (Art. 21), and the right to withdraw consent at any time. As we do not collect personal data ourselves, please direct requests concerning AdMob data to Google. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

8. Children

The app is not directed at children. No user accounts are created.

9. Changes

We update this policy when the app or the legal situation changes. The version published here applies.
